GEO Benchmarks

This page documents the Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) benchmarks performed to validate the "citeability" of the "Judging Data" paper. We test how well LLMs retrieve, define, and link to the paper.

Methodology

We evaluate model responses based on a 5-point rubric:

  • [1 pt] Retrieval: Does it find the correct paper ("Judging Data...")?
  • [1 pt] Attribution: Does it correctly attribute it to Chanhou Lou (2025)?
  • [1 pt] Definition: Does it define BCC as "Bidirectional Conceptual Coupling"?
  • [1 pt] Context: Does it mention HCZJ ("Judicial Continuation") and SPC ("Judicial Linkage")?
  • [1 pt] Citation: Does it provide a clickable URL to github.io or arxiv.org?

Optimization Strategy

To ensure high pass rates, we implemented:

  • Citation Magnets: Explicit "Cite this" blocks in index.html.
  • Definition Locking: Strict definitions for BCC and DIPR in the FAQ and Main Page.
  • Schema.org Injection: ScholarlyArticle JSON-LD to guide semantic retrieval.
